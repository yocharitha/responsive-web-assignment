Why is that?

Many Bootstrap websites start their life as a mockup in Photoshop.

When designing for Bootstrap, it comes in handy to have the targeted column sizes available in some form, so objects and text can be aligned to the grid easily.

Do I know this?

For Bootstrap 3, there’s a myriad of good templates, but for the upcoming Bootstrap 4 there is not much around yet.

This is why I created this Bootstrap 4 Grid PSD as a foundation to quickly mockup a theme or website

Do I like it?

Artboards were recently introduced in Photoshop CC and are very useful if you want to mockup your website for various screen sizes.

I’ve added artboards for all Bootstrap breakpoints except xs.
